Tofu Nuggets

Ingredients 1 package extra firm tofu For best results, freeze overnight, thaw (will take most of the day on the counter), and drain (press with a clean towel or with a tofu press to remove water) Cut into nugget-sized bites 1/2 cup bread crumbs Panko works well for extra crunch We typically use Italian Panko bread crumbs. Be mindful of ingredients because many bread crumbs contain eggs 2 tbsp corn starch 2 tbps nutritional yeast 1 tsp salt 1/8 tsp cracked black pepper 1/4 flour We use whole wheat flour.

Tofu Sweet Potato Curry

Ingredients 1 package extra firm tofu, drained and cubed 1 medium onion 4 garlic cloves minced 1/2 tsp ginger 1 sweet potato 1 russet potato 2 carrots 2 tbsp red curry paste 2 tsp Thai curry seasoning 1 tbsp peanut butter 2 tbsp maple syrup 3 tbsp lime juice 3 tbsp fish sauce 1 can of coconut milk 1/2 cup vegetable broth Instructions In a small bowl, set aside red curry paste, Thai curry seasoning, and peanut butter In a small bowl, mix together and set aside maple syrup, lime juice, and fish sauce Saute onion, garlic, and ginger in pressure cooker for about 2 mins Add 1/2 cup vegetable broth and stir Add red curry paste mixture; stir until well incorporated Add maple syrup mixture and stir Add coconut milk and stir Add potatoes and tofu and stir Pressure cook for 6 mins

Vegan Cheese Sauce

Ingredients 2 cups potatoes, diced large 1 cup carrots, diced large 1/2 cup water 1/3 cup olive oil 2 tsp salt 1 tbsp lemon juice 1/2 cup nutritional yeast 1/4 tsp onion powder 1/4 tsp garlic powder Instructions Boil the potatoes and carrots until soft Blend potatoes and carrots together along with the rest of the ingredients on high until the cheese is smooth Store in refrigerator for up to a week
