Nacho Cheese Sauce

July 25, 2020

Ingredients

  • 1 cup raw cashews (soaked)
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1 tbsp unsweetened almond milk
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 2 tbsp pickled jalapenos + 2 tbsp pickled jalapeno brine

Instructions

  1. Soak cashews overnight (or in boiling water for 1 hour)
  2. Drain soaked cashews and pat dry
  3. Add all ingredients to a high-powered blender
  4. Blend until thick, creamy, and smooth
