Vegan Cheese Sauce

Ingredients 2 cups potatoes, diced large 1 cup carrots, diced large 1/2 cup water 1/3 cup olive oil 2 tsp salt 1 tbsp lemon juice 1/2 cup nutritional yeast 1/4 tsp onion powder 1/4 tsp carlic powder Instructions Boil the potatoes and carrots until soft Blend potatoes and carrots together along with the rest of the ingredients on high until the cheese is smooth Store in refrigerator for up to a week

Nacho Cheese Sauce

Ingredients 1 cup raw cashews (soaked) 1/2 cup salsa 1 tbsp unsweetened almond milk 2 tbsp water 1 tsp ground cumin 1/2 tsp chili powder 1/2 tsp smoked paprika 1 tsp kosher salt 2 tbsp nutritional yeast 2 tbsp pickled jalapenos + 2 tbsp pickled jalapeno brine Instructions Soak cashews overnight (or in boiling water for 1 hour) Drain soaked cashews and pat dry Add all ingredients to a high-powered blender Blend until thick, creamy, and smooth

Banana Oat Pancakes

Ingredients 1 rounded cup bananas (sliced, ~1.5 bananas) 1 cup quick oats 1 tsp baking powder 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil 2 tbsp maple syrup 3/4 cup + 3 tbsp non-dairy milk 1/4 tsp sea salt Instructions Blend all ingredients until smooth (long enough to get air into batter) Use 1/4 cup batter for each pancake Add non-dairy milk if batter gets too thick Flip pancakes when bubbles start to form around edges

Tofu Scramble

Ingredients 1/2 sweet onion (thinly sliced) 1/2 to 2/3 red bell pepper (sliced) 5-6 oz. white mushrooms (sliced) 2/3 to 1 block of extra firm regular tofu 2 tbsp+ extra virgin olive oil separated 2 tsp+ tamari (or soy sauce) separated 1/2 tsp tumeric 1/8 tsp sea salt Instructions Tofu Preparation Squeeze out tofu. You can press the tofu, but it isn’t absolutely necessary Crumble tofu into bowl Add 1/2 rounded tsp tumeric Add 1/8 tsp sea salt Add 1 tsp tamari (or soy sauce) Add 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil Mash tofu until it has the consistency of scrambled eggs Cooking Caramelize onions with 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil on medium heat (~5 minutes); add 1 tbsp water as needed When onions are golden and translucent, add mushrooms Drizzle tamari (or soy sauce) and extra virgin olive oil over mushrooms Cook down mushrooms for ~5 minutes Add red pepper and 1 tsp of tamari (or soy sauce) Cook for ~3 minutes before adding tofu Mix in tofu Let brown on bottom 1-2 minutes.
