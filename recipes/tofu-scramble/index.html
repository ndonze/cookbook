Tofu Scramble

May 13, 2020

Ingredients

  • 1/2 sweet onion (thinly sliced)
  • 1/2 to 2/3 red bell pepper (sliced)
  • 5-6 oz. white mushrooms (sliced)
  • 2/3 to 1 block of extra firm regular tofu
  • 2 tbsp+ extra virgin olive oil separated
  • 2 tsp+ tamari (or soy sauce) separated
  • 1/2 tsp tumeric
  • 1/8 tsp sea salt

Instructions

Tofu Preparation

  1. Squeeze out tofu. You can press the tofu, but it isn’t absolutely necessary
  2. Crumble tofu into bowl
  3. Add 1/2 rounded tsp tumeric
  4. Add 1/8 tsp sea salt
  5. Add 1 tsp tamari (or soy sauce)
  6. Add 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  7. Mash tofu until it has the consistency of scrambled eggs

Cooking

  1. Caramelize onions with 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil on medium heat (~5 minutes); add 1 tbsp water as needed
  2. When onions are golden and translucent, add mushrooms
  3. Drizzle tamari (or soy sauce) and extra virgin olive oil over mushrooms
  4. Cook down mushrooms for ~5 minutes
  5. Add red pepper and 1 tsp of tamari (or soy sauce)
  6. Cook for ~3 minutes before adding tofu
  7. Mix in tofu
  8. Let brown on bottom 1-2 minutes. Flip and cook for 1-2 minutes
