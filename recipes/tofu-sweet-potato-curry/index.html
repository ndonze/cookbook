Tofu Sweet Potato Curry

June 1, 2021

Ingredients

  • 1 package extra firm tofu, drained and cubed
  • 1 medium onion
  • 4 garlic cloves minced
  • 1/2 tsp ginger
  • 1 sweet potato
  • 1 russet potato
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 tbsp red curry paste
  • 2 tsp Thai curry seasoning
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 3 tbsp lime juice
  • 3 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable broth

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, set aside red curry paste, Thai curry seasoning, and peanut butter
  2. In a small bowl, mix together and set aside maple syrup, lime juice, and fish sauce
  3. Saute onion, garlic, and ginger in pressure cooker for about 2 mins
  4. Add 1/2 cup vegetable broth and stir
  5. Add red curry paste mixture; stir until well incorporated
  6. Add maple syrup mixture and stir
  7. Add coconut milk and stir
  8. Add potatoes and tofu and stir
  9. Pressure cook for 6 mins
